Meta Ads D2C Fashion E-Commerce

D2C Fashion Brand Launch — Full-Funnel Meta Ads Campaign

Client: Picks For Youu (PFY) — Indian Fusion Wear & Western fashion startup · 2-month-old brand

02

Objective: Build brand awareness, drive Instagram profile visits, and generate DM-based sales enquiries for a newly launched D2C fashion brand blending Indian Fusion Wear and Western styles.

Designed and managed a full-funnel, 3-campaign Meta Ads architecture across 4 days: Traffic Campaign (Instagram page visits, ABO with 2 ad sets), Sales Campaign (UGC + testimonial creatives), and Leads Campaign (product video for DM intent). Used Ad Set Budget Optimisation (ABO) to control spend at the creative level and test performance independently.

Outcome: Best creative achieved ₹17.38 CPL with 24 messaging conversions. Campaign generated a confirmed sale — real revenue from paid spend. Total campaign reach: 2,06,618 users on ₹2,400 budget.

Campaign Architecture

3 campaigns, 4 days, ₹2,500 total: Traffic (ABO, 2 ad sets, ₹150/day × 3 days), Sales (UGC + testimonials, ₹500 lifetime), Leads (product video, ₹500/day × 2 days). Each campaign tested a different funnel objective.

Audience Strategy

Creative 1 targeted broad engaged shoppers (170M–200M, all genders, India) — maximising reach for brand awareness. Creative 2 narrowed to fashion-forward women (18–55) with trendsetters and wedding interests for higher purchase intent.

Creative Approach

Sales campaign used UGC + customer testimonials to build social proof and trust for a 2-month-old brand. Leads campaign used an informational product video to introduce the range and motivate DM enquiries — message-optimised placements.

💡

Business-level read: ₹2,500 spend → 2,06,618 reach → 50+ leads → 1 confirmed sale. At an average fashion AOV of ₹600–₹1,200, even a 10% lead-to-sale conversion rate (5 sales × ₹800 avg) = ₹4,000 revenue on ₹2,500 spend — a 1.6× ROAS on a cold-traffic brand launch with zero prior audience. The next step is Meta Pixel setup + retargeting audiences for 3–5× ROAS in phase 2.
